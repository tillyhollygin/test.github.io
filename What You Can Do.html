It is easy to dissociate from the effects of Climate Change. 
     It is even easier to make a few changes and help the planet out!



ACTION

REASON

Use Reusable Coffee Cups
This saves single-use plastic waste from ending up in the oceans. It also saves you money; as many coffee shops will give you a discount!
Support a Charity
You could adopt an endangered animal through WWF or volunteer or fund-raise for an environmental charity like GREENPEACE
Avoid Palm Oil
Palm Oil production is fueling deforestation. Try to avoid products that contain it to reduce the demand and inform yourself at Saynotopalmoil.com


If we want the future generations to coexist with these diverse and captivating wild species then we must make a change. Before it is too late.
